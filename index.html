FAQ

How do I put parental controls on an iPhone for YouTube?

To set parental controls on an iPhone for YouTube, you can set Age Restrictions, set a Screen Time Limit, and activate Restrict Mode.

How do I restrict inappropriate content on YouTube?

To prevent access to any inappropriate content on YouTube, you just have to enable the Restrict Mode. Tap Settings >> General >> Restricted Mode.

Does iPhone have a kid mode?

Yes, there’s a kid mode on an iPhone. To enable it, open Settings >> General >> Accessibility >> Guided Access and turn on the toggle for “Guided Access.”
